Step 1 of 4

Find live briefs before they close

The Procurement Notices tab shows councils and public bodies actively seeking architects, masterplanners and development partners. Notices are sorted by deadline — the most urgent at the top.

Step 2 of 4

Track major applications by stage

The Planning Pipeline tab surfaces large-scale applications — 50+ homes — across England. Outline permissions come first: a site with outline planning but no reserved matters means a developer needs an architect.

Step 3 of 4

Find sites with no developer yet

The Brownfield Land tab shows allocated sites from England's official register. Sites marked "No Permission Yet" are the most speculative — land the council wants developed but no one has optioned.

Step 4 of 4

Search and filter by what matters to you

Use the search bar to find opportunities by location, authority or keyword — try "masterplan", "Southwark" or "outline". Filter by region and scale. Click any result to see full details and zoom the map.
